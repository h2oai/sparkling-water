##v1.3.0 (2015-05-25)
  - Major release of Sparkling Water
  - Depends on:
    - Spark 1.3.1
    - H2O 3.0 Shannon release
  - It contains major renaming of API: 
    - H2O's DataFrame was renamed to H2OFrame
    - Spark's SchemaRDD was renamed to DataFrame

##v1.2.0 (2015-05-18)
  - Major release of Sparkling Water
  - Depends on:

    - H2O 3.0 Shannon release
